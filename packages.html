Basic

The Basic Digital Marketing Package is for small businesses looking for top-of-the-funnel marketing support. It includes 35 digital marketing points per month, a monthly playbook, a KPI center updated bi-weekly, and a live dashboard. Clients work directly with marketing specialists and an account coordinator/manager.

Pro

The Pro Package is a digital marketing package designed for small to medium-sized companies to drive growth with marketing consulting and services. It includes 74 digital marketing points per month, task-by-task transparency, and dedicated specialists.

Growth 

The Growth Package provides medium-sized companies with an outsourced creative, web, and digital marketing team to drive growth. It includes 154 digital marketing service points per month, an integrated marketing plan, and an online campaign management center. Add-on features are also included at no extra cost.

Enterprise 

The Enterprise Edition provides fully integrated digital marketing and advertising campaigns across the funnel, including growth hacking for immediate KPI growth. Dedicated full-time staff, including a digital marketing manager and specialists, are available at all times and can work at the client's facility a couple of times a week.
